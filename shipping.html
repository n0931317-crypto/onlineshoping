Shipping Methods

We use the following courier services for delivering your orders:

  • Nepal Post: Reliable domestic and international shipping
  • Daraz Logistics: Fast delivery within Nepal
  • Private Courier Services: For urgent orders (if applicable)
  • DHL/FedEx: For international shipments

Tracking information will be provided once your order ships.

Processing Time

⏱️ Order Processing: 1-2 business days

After you place an order, we begin processing it within 24 hours. You will receive a confirmation email with tracking details once your package is shipped.

Note: Processing time does not include weekends and public holidays.

Delivery Guidelines

  • Packages are delivered to the address provided during checkout
  • Delivery attempts are made during business hours (9 AM - 6 PM)
  • If no one is available, the courier will leave a notification
  • You can request a redelivery at no extra cost
  • Signature may be required for high-value orders

Shipping Restrictions

Please note the following restrictions for certain countries:

  • Some cosmetic products may have restrictions in specific countries
  • We follow all international regulations for product shipment
  • Liquid products may have additional shipping requirements
  • Certain countries may require customs declaration

Package Tracking

You can track your package in real-time:

  • Tracking link will be sent via email
  • Use our Track Order page
  • Updates are provided at each stage of delivery
  • Contact us for manual tracking assistance

Damaged or Lost Orders

If your order arrives damaged or is lost in transit:

  • Report within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos of the damaged package and product
  • Include your order number and tracking ID
  • We will file a claim with the courier immediately
  • Replacement or refund will be issued (typically within 5-7 days)

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does shipping take?

A: Standard shipping within Nepal takes 3-5 business days, Express takes 1-2 days, and international shipments take 7-15 business days depending on the destination country.

Q: Is there free shipping?

A: Yes! Orders over Rs. 2,000 qualify for free standard shipping. Otherwise, shipping costs start from Rs. 50.

Q: Can I change my delivery address after ordering?

A: You can change the address within 12 hours of placing the order. Contact us immediately if you need to make changes.

Q: What if my package is delayed?

A: If your package is delayed beyond the estimated delivery date, contact us immediately. We will track it and follow up with the courier.

Q: Do you ship internationally?

A: Yes! We ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates vary based on destination and weight. Calculate your shipping cost at checkout.

Q: How is my package protected?

A: All packages are carefully packed with protective materials. For fragile items, we use extra padding and sturdy boxes to ensure safe delivery.
